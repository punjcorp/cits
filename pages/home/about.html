Consult for IT Solutions is a startup software company founded by Amit Sharma who has experience of more than 10 years working in Multi National Companies for many clients in USA. He has setup this IT firm along with his colleagues and close friends who share the same ideas and aims in life. The aim behind this company setup is to provide good quality IT Solutions for common people which can help improve daily lifestyle of the users.

Our founder and core team brings more than 45+ years of experience in Information Technology Industry. The core team has developed many solutions for a vast group of domains in this time. Our highly skilled and professional team has End-to-End experience about Retail, Fashion, Telecommunication, Payment Systems and Devices, Transportation and Logistics, Banking and many more. Our team has worked with various clients in different countries which helps us understand various business and unique requirements at very detail level for created the best solution suited for our client's needs.

The goal of this startup is to make the lifestyle easy for the users rather than creating a digital solution which has no benefit for the users. Now a days, everyone is going digital. A very few people care about the solution delivered to them or whether the delivered solution is doing any good to the users. Most of the solutions are sold to the users but are useless in a very short period of time. The main reason for these useless solutions is lack of knowledge and short sightedness of the requirements while developing. As a startup company, our main focus is going to be on inception of the needs for our clients. We will ensure to listen, discuss and consult all the factors before the decision to develop a digital solution is made for our clients.

Our core team has seen a lot of educated people in our surroundings who avoid using computers for manual tasks because they are not able to adapt to the technology changes in their daily work. We want to help, support and educate people in transitioning to new era of digital world. Where the hours of manual work can be easy completed in a few minutes with the help of digital solutions.

We are launching a few solutions soon which will help our clients. We are launching GST enabled Point of Sales Solution and working on a Society (Apartment) Management Solution. These solutions were picked based on the needs seen in our local market and the problems faced by clients in implementing the already available solutions in market.
